About

I’m Victor, and I’ve been passionate about video games since I was very young, and that passion has stayed with me into adulthood. For a long time, I didn’t know how to turn that passion into a career until recently. I enrolled in The Tech Academy, a coding bootcamp that helped guide my love for gaming in the right direction.

There is something powerful about creating something from nothing and turning an idea into reality. It truly feels like bringing a dream to life. Realizing that I could actually build games myself is what led me to pursue a career as a video game developer.
